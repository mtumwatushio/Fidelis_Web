About JR Institute of Information Technology

The institution was established in 1999 when JR Electronics LTD, a company dealing with IT communication and network branched itself to give a start to an education entity.

VISION

Training youths who are unable to pursue advanced secondary education acquire appropriate quality technical skills through holistic development

MOTTO

Bridging the gap of education by creating opportunities through Business Development

MISSION

Developing participants into various skills in line with present and future requirements of the Tanzania labour market. Producing modern day professionals with high skills of entrepreneurship and professionalism to see Tanzania through challenge of the 21st century.
